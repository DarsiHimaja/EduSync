About EduSync

EduSync is a Generative AI–powered curriculum design system that automates the creation of structured courses, topic plans, and learning outcomes based on educational requirements. It empowers educators to build industry-aligned academic programs efficiently — saving hours of planning time while producing high-quality, personalized learning paths.

Our Mission

🎓 Empowering Educators

Reduce the time educators spend on curriculum planning so they can focus on what matters most — teaching.

🌐 Industry Alignment

Generate curricula that reflect the latest industry trends, tools, and standards in every domain.

Key Features

  • ✅   JWT-secured user authentication
  • ✅   AI-generated week-by-week study plans
  • ✅   Measurable learning outcomes per course
  • ✅   Curated resource recommendations (videos, courses, books)
  • ✅   Assessment ideas for educators
  • ✅   Support for Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced levels
